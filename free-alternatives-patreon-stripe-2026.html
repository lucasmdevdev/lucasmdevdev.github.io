5 Free Alternatives to Stripe/Patreon That Work in 2026

1. Liberapay (Open Source, Ethical)

What: Donations platform, no CAPTCHA, no ads

✓ Zero fees, open source, recurring donations

✗ Smaller audience

Best for: Open source projects, ethical creators

2. Ko-fi (Creator-Friendly)

What: Donations + shop + courses

✓ Free tier, 5% fees, built-in shop

✗ Limited customization

Best for: Creators, artists, developers

3. GitHub Sponsors (For Developers)

What: Integrated into GitHub, no setup fees

✓ 0% fees, organic discovery, trusted platform

✗ GitHub-only audience

Best for: Open source maintainers

4. Substack (Newsletter Monetization)

What: Newsletter platform with payments

✓ Free tier, 10% on paid subscriptions, email list building

✗ Content locked in their platform

Best for: Writers, thought leaders

5. Bitcoin/Crypto Donations

What: Wallet address in bio

✓ Zero fees, instant settlement, no CAPTCHA

✗ Requires crypto knowledge

Best for: Tech audience
