Freelancing as a Developer: Complete Guide

Set Your Rate

$50-150/hour for freelancers (starting)
$150-300/hour for experienced developers

Too low = burnout. Too high = no clients. Start at $75-100.

Find Clients

  • Upwork (best for starting)
  • Fiverr (service-based)
  • Network (highest paying)
  • Referrals (easiest)

Deliver Quality

5-star reviews = exponential growth.
One bad client = 10x bad reviews.

Scale Up

At $5K/month → Hire junior dev
At $10K/month → Build agency
At $20K/month → Automate & build SaaS

Freelancing is your first step to building wealth as a developer.
